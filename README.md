# Bulk SMS Nigeria

A developer-friendly resource explaining how businesses use Bulk SMS to automate customer communication, transactional alerts, OTP verification, appointment reminders, and marketing campaigns.

Bulk SMS remains one of the most reliable communication channels because it works on virtually every mobile phone without requiring an internet connection. Businesses across banking, healthcare, education, logistics, e-commerce, and retail continue to use SMS to reach customers instantly.

## Why Businesses Use Bulk SMS

Bulk SMS helps organizations automate communication while improving customer engagement. Common use cases include:

- Promotional SMS campaigns
- Transactional notifications
- OTP verification
- Appointment reminders
- Payment confirmations
- Delivery updates
- Customer support notifications
- Internal employee communication
